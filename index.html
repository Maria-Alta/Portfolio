About me

I've always had a natural curiosity and passion for learning, whether it's exploring different topics or diving deep into a challenge. Coding has been an interest of mine since my teenage years, but for a long time, I believed it was too difficult to learn to code or that it was too late to start. However, I recently decided to take the plunge and begin my journey into the world of software development.


I came across a quote that resonates with me: "It always seems impossible until it's done." That perfectly describes my mindset right now. I've started by learning HTML and CSS, and these are my first projects, created as part of my first certification on freeCodeCamp. While web development is where I'm beginning, it's just the start—I don't plan to stop here. My goal is to go beyond websites and dive deeper into more complex areas of software development.


I come from a diverse background that includes economics, education, and creative pursuits like music and writing. These experiences have helped me to balance analytical thinking and creativity. As I dive deeper into coding, I’m finding new ways to apply these strengths to my work in software development.


Though I’m just starting out, I’m excited to continue growing my skills and exploring new areas in the tech world. I’ll be sharing my progress along the way, and I look forward to seeing where this journey takes me. If you're interested in viewing my personal art projects, feel free to check out my LinkTree.
